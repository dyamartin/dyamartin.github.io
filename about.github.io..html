ABOUT

Dya Martin was born in Pitesti, Romania and moved to Las Vegas, United States in 2014.
She studied Painting and Sculpture at "Dinu Lipatti' Fine Arts High School and later graduated with a bachelors from University of Science in Romania. Dya had collective sculpture exhibitions in high school and worked in the fashion industry in Romania for many years as a model, fashion designer, but also as an Art Director and Fashion Show organizer for local designers. She has an eye for clothing, stage, lights and everything that is connected to the spectacle's world.. She worked as a Radio Producer and host for the local radio Muntenia FM where she was conducting her own Fashion Talk Show. Dya has always had an interest and burning curiosity with regards to cinematography and the art of acting and she took one-on-one acting lessons with an established Romanian actor. She appeared in Romanian music videos and national and international commercials. She writes poetry in both Romanian and English and she is a polyglot and speaks: Romanian, English, French, Italian, Spanish and lately she has developed interest in Turkish. 
While continuing to focus on her Modeling career and working in the art field as an Artist Representative for a well known contemporary artist, Dya landed her first opportunity in the fashion industry in US from a notorious New York Couture Designer when she was offered to be the image of the brand. During the past years she has got involved in few charitable events and she desires to continue on this path.
Dya is also a self-taught dancer and skilled herself in: Contemporary, Bollywood, Modern, House, Belly Dance and just recently she has started studying  Argentinian Tango. She has experience in photography, web design and digital marketing. Horseback riding is another old passion of  Dya and she has a special connection with horses.
Dya Martin continues to expand her horizons in acting and beyond acting which involves screenwriting, filmmaking and film directing. She is a perfectionist and a determined artist willing to push her limits that will contribute to her evolution as a successful actor and cinematographer in Hollywood.

"For me, I see filmmaking as art" 

Emily Browning
